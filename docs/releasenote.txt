Just Art is an art gallery application, everything is updated from The Art Institute of Chicago
With this app, you can browse and bookmark your favourite artwork. You can share it with friend on social media like Facebook or Twitter

- Infinite scrolling to see every artworks that are featured now
- Search artworks help you search artworks from your favourite artist
- Share artworks to social media and to your friend including the image of the artwork

We release this first version to give you the initial experience of this app, we also look forward to hearing feedback from you
